The Ink Remembers

Catch the words before the past forgets them.

How to Play

  1. A letter appears from a witness. Read fast — the ink starts fading almost right away.
  2. Click or tap a word before it disappears to catch it. Caught words are safe for good.
  3. Once the whole letter has faded or been caught, the next witness's account appears.
  4. After both letters, a corkboard shows what you managed to piece together — anything you missed appears as an ink blot instead.
  5. Clue words change colour when caught.
  6. Keep going through all five memories. What you catch adds up — catch enough, and the final letter reveals the truth.
